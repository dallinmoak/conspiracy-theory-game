More than 50% of Americans believe in at least one conspiracy theory.

Men are slightly more likely to believe in conspiracy theories than women, especially when it comes to political conspiracies.

A 2020 poll showed that the top three most popular conspiracy theories in the U.S. are:

  • Aliens exist and the government is hiding the evidence.
  • The JFK assassination was a cover-up.
  • The 9/11 attacks were an inside job.
